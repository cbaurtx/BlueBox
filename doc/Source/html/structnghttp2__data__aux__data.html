◆ flags

uint8_t nghttp2_data_aux_data::flags

The flags of DATA frame. We use separate flags here and nghttp2_data frame. The latter contains flags actually sent to peer. This |flags| may contain NGHTTP2_FLAG_END_STREAM and only when |eof| becomes nonzero, flags in nghttp2_data has NGHTTP2_FLAG_END_STREAM set.
